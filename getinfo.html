Get PrEP Info

Whether you've never heard of PrEP or you've heard a lot, these pages are here to help you get accurate information about PrEP.

What is PrEP?

The What is Prep page is a great place to start if you don't know anything about PrEP at all, or if you feel like the information you've heard so far isn't completely accurate. Check out the video!

Myths and Misinformation

Maybe you've heard PrEP is super expensive and only really rich people can get it. Or that there are all kinds of crazy side effects. This page seeks to clear about a lot of the myths and misinformation out there about PrEP.

What PrEP Users Have to Say

People take PrEP for all kinds of reasons and have all kinds of different experiences taking PrEP. Check out what some PrEP users have to say about their experiences.
