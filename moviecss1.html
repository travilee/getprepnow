The Devil Wears Prada

Based on the bestselling book by Lauren Weisberger, this is the story of a young writer who needs to find a job to break into the world of print in the big city of New York. She lands the job "a million girls would kill for," but soon discovers she's working for a dragon lady. The film features great one-liners, beautiful fashions, and a heartwarming storyline.

Main Cast

  • Anne Hathaway - Andy Sachs
  • Emily Blunt - Emily
  • Stanley Tucci - Nigel

  • Adrian Grenier - Nate
