Homemade Pasta Salad

This pasta salad recipe is easy to follow, delicious to eat, and inexpensive to buy. Make it for your next family get-together!

Ingredients

  • Tri-Color Rotini Pasta, 12 oz box
  • Italian Salad Dressing, 16 oz bottle
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Medium Pitted Black Olives, 1 can
  • Artichoke Hearts, 1 can
  • Sharp and/or Mild Cheddar Cheese, cubed
  • Parmesan Cheese, Grated
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

Preparation Steps

  1. Using a medium-sized pot, bring water to a boil for pasta (see pasta box for amount of suggested water).
  2. Stir pasta into boiling water. Boil pasta for 12 minutes or until soft. Stir frequently while boiling.
  3. Open can of olives, drain water, and pour olives into a large bowl.
  4. Open can of artichoke hearts, drain water, and cut hearts into quarters or bite-size pieces. Put pieces into bowl.
  5. Cut cherry tomatoes into smaller pieces if desired and put in bowl.
  6. Put cheddar cheese cubes in bowl.
  7. When pasta is ready, dump into strainer, shake off hot water, and rinse pasta for about 30 seconds with cool water. Shake off excess water and then dump pasta into bowl with other ingredients.
  8. Before mixing, pour about half the bottle of Italian dressing evenly over the top of the pasta. Sprinkle desired amount of garlic powder and Italian seasoning on top of dressing, and then sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the dressing.
  9. Stir ingredients in bowl together, ensuring dressing evenly coats all ingredients.
  10. Add more dressing, seasonings, and parmesan cheese as needed for flavoring.
  11. Pasta salad can be served immediately after preparation, or can be chilled prior to serving.
