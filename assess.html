Figure Out if PrEP is Right for You

Taking PrEP is a great idea if you want to lower your risk of getting HIV. However, it's not recommended that everyone be on PrEP.

Use the questions below to determine if PrEP might be right for you.

If you answer "yes" to one or more of the questions, PrEP might be a good idea for you. It's strongly recommended you talk with a doctor or other clinician about your reasons for wanting to take PrEP.

PrEP Self-Assessment
Question Yes No
Are you HIV-negative?* Yes No
Do you currently have an HIV-positive sexual partner? Yes No
Have you had sex with more than one partner in the last 12 months? Yes No
Have you had anal or vaginal sex in the last 12 months and on one or more occasion not used condoms? Yes No

*PrEP is not appropriate treatment for HIV. If you are HIV-positive, talk with your doctor/clinician about HIV treatment.
